Our Donate Plan

We are mainly focusing on giving nutrient food, shelter and medicine to the flood effected people using your donation money.

Food for flood people

Flood in Sylhet destroyed existing food rations, agriculture crops and livestock, leading to severe shortages. We are planning to use a portion of donation money to provide them nutrient foods. These nutrient foods will give them energy to fight against water caused diseases and to remain strong.

Shelter for homeless

According to the Sylhet District administration, there are now 439 shelters in Sylhet district where 37,176 people are still staying in these shelters. Almost 3 million people have been rendered homeless and as many as 40,091 houses destroyed in the recent floods in Sylhet, sources said on Thursday.

Medicines

Many people are suffer from water caused diseases like cholera, diarrhoea, dysentery, hepatitis A, typhoid and polio etc. Moreover, there are older persons who need regular medicine to maintain their health. With your donation these people can get proper medicines.
